# 1 Peter

**Author:** Peter  
**Date:** ~64-68 AD  
**Theme:** Living as Holy Strangers  
**Key Verse:** 1 Peter 1:3 - "Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who according to His abundant mercy has begotten us again to a living hope through the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead"

---

## Overview

First Peter is a general epistle written by the apostle Peter to believers scattered throughout Asia Minor. The letter encourages Christians to remain faithful amid persecution while living as holy strangers in a hostile world.
